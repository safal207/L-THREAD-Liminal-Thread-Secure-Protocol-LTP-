## What LTP Is
LTP is a deterministic, explainable transport protocol for routing intent,
orientation, and contextual state between clients, agents, and systems.

## What LTP Is NOT
- Not a recommendation system
- Not an AI model
- Not a datastore
- Not a framework

## When to Use LTP
- DevTools & Observability
- Fintech routing & decision flows
- Context-aware AI agents
- Explainable multi-path systems

## When NOT to Use LTP
- CRUD APIs
- Simple request/response
- Stateless microservices
