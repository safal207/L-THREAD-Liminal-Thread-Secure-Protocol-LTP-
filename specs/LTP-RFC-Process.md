# LTP RFC Process

## RFC Types
- Informational
- Standards Track
- Experimental

## Lifecycle
1. Draft (Pull Request)
2. Community Discussion
3. Revision
4. Acceptance or Rejection
5. Status Freeze

## Decision Making
LTP uses rough consensus:
A proposal is accepted when:
- major objections are addressed
- no unresolved blocking concerns remain
- at least two independent implementations are possible

> Reference implementations MAY exist, but MUST NOT define the protocol.
